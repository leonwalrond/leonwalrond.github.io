About us

Who are Banna Technologies?

Banna Technologies have realised that with the ever increasing growth of urbanisation and associated demand for resources, infrastructure and transportation, there is a need for a multi-disciplinary consultancy to support the delivery of turnkey solutions for projects and their lifecycles. Our team of consultants have a wealth of experience in the realms of design, engineering, procurement and construction disciplines. Through our global industry networks, we have established strategic partnerships across continents which have allowed us the ability to provide technical support, advice and value engineering to our clients to support their successful execution of significant engineering projects. Through our people, Banna Technologies offers a high quality and reliable service to the national and international market.
